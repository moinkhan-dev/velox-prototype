Our Story

More Than Just a Meal

Three branches across Delhi NCR. One family. A passion for food that brings people together.

Velox Restro & Cafe was born from a simple belief — that great food brings people together. What started as a small dream in Daryaganj has grown into three beloved branches across Delhi NCR, each serving the same authentic North Indian, Chinese, and Arabian cuisine that made Velox a household name.

Every dish at Velox is crafted with fresh ingredients, time-honoured recipes, and a deep respect for tradition. From our signature Velox Special to the slow-cooked Champaran Mutton Handi and the legendary Arabian Mandi — each plate tells a story of passion, quality, and love for food.

Our chefs bring decades of combined experience, blending traditional techniques with contemporary presentation. We source the finest ingredients, ensuring every bite reflects our commitment to quality. Whether you're dining in with family, ordering for a celebration, or grabbing a quick bite — Velox promises an experience that lingers long after the last bite.
